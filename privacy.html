Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Overview

Unpissed helps people find, register and rate bathrooms. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and what choices users have.

Data We Collect

Depending on how you use Unpissed, we may collect:

  • Account information, such as your email address, display name and profile image.
  • Google sign-in information provided by Google, such as your name, email address and avatar.
  • Bathroom submissions, check-ins, ratings, comments, badges, follows and reports you create in the app.
  • Approximate or precise location while you are actively using location-based features.
  • Uploaded bathroom photos, if you choose to add them.
  • Basic technical data needed to run the app, such as authentication sessions and request metadata.

How We Use Data

We use this data to:

  • Sign you in and keep your account secure.
  • Show nearby bathrooms, ratings and map markers.
  • Save your check-ins, badge progress, bathroom submissions and friend connections.
  • Moderate reports, unverified bathroom data and uploaded content.
  • Improve the reliability and usefulness of the app.

Location Data

Location is used to sort bathrooms near you, show routes and place new bathroom submissions on the map. Location access is requested by your browser or device. Unpissed does not need background location access. You can disable location access in your browser or device settings.

Google Sign-In

If you use Google sign-in, Google provides authentication data such as your Google account identifier, email address, name and profile image. Unpissed uses this only to create or access your Unpissed account. We do not sell Google user data or use it for advertising.

Sharing and Visibility

Public bathroom data, ratings, badges and non-anonymous activity may be visible to other users. Anonymous check-ins are designed to hide your name in public activity. Friend features may show limited profile and activity information to other users.

Service Providers

Unpissed uses third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, storage and app backend services.
  • Google for optional Google sign-in.
  • OpenStreetMap-related map services for map tiles and geocoding features.

Retention and Deletion

We keep account and app activity data for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain safety and improve bathroom data quality. You may request deletion of your account-related data by contacting the project owner.

Security

Unpissed uses Supabase authentication and row-level security rules to protect account data. No system is perfectly secure, but the app is designed to limit access to personal data and user-owned records.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact the Unpissed project owner through the public project channel at github.com/gomezaka/Unpissed/issues.
